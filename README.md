# About Our Plugins
All DeathCore plugins are designed with performance and usability in mind. They are lightweight, easy to use, and fully compatible with each other, meaning they work seamlessly together.  

---

## Specific Plugins

<details>
  <summary>EventCore</summary>
  
**Features:**
- Event management system with timers and cooldowns  
- Player life management (revive, kill, revive tokens)  
- Teleports and warps  
- Arena management with block control, lava raising, and floor clearing  
- Quick toggles for PVP, block placing/breaking, and storage access  
- Host/permissions system integrated with LuckPerms  

</details>

<details>
  <summary>Login Security</summary>
  
**Features:**
- Player registration and login system  
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) support  
- Password management  
- Settings GUI for managing 2FA and passwords  
- Prevents unlogged players from interacting with the world  

</details>
